About CRC

Part of the Christian Revival Church movement founded in 1994. We believe in the authority of the Bible, Trinity, salvation by faith, Holy Spirit baptism, divine healing, and holiness.

Our mission: Win the lost at any cost, mend nets, build one church in many locations.

We welcome all races, ages, and backgrounds to worship with us.
